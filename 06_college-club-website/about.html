About Us

The GameOn Club is a vibrant student community passionate about competitive and casual gaming. Our mission is to bring together like-minded individuals who enjoy everything from classic arcade battles to modern multiplayer tournaments.

Founded in 2020, the club has grown into a recognized hub for tech enthusiasts, strategists, and creatives. We host regular LAN parties, workshops, and intercollegiate competitions to boost skills and foster teamwork.

Whether you're a seasoned gamer or just curious about the scene, you'll find a place here to learn, play, and connect.
